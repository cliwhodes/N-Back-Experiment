N-Back Position Test

A standardized memory test — the same sequence runs for every participant, so your results can be compared.

How It Works

A square will light up in one of the 9 positions on the grid. Each trial lasts 4 seconds. Your task is simple:

Press the button whenever the current position matches where the square was 2 trials ago.

A
Press A (or the button) when the highlighted square is in the same position as it was 2 turns back. Press nothing if it does not match.

The first 2 trials have no correct answer — just watch. Try to respond only when you are sure, as wrong presses count against your score.
